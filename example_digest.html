mistralai/Mixtral-8x22B-v0.1

License: Apache-2.0

Hardware Verdict: Runs well quantized (Q4) on CPU, expect moderate generation speed. GGUF version available.

Benchmark Note: Outperforms previous Mistral models on several benchmarks, showing significant gains in reasoning and coding tasks.
